About Kepha Sports Entertainment Club;

Welcome to Kepha Sports Entertainment Club, your ultimate destination for all things such as Sports and Entertainment established with a passion for promoting athleticism, teamwork, and a love for Sports. Our Club brings together enthusiasts of football, basketball, rugby, hockey and tennis under one exciting umbrella.

At Kepha Sports Entertainment Club, our mission is to foster a sense of community and camaraderie through Sports. We are dedicated to;

  • Promote Sports excellence
  • Bring people together
  • Promote entertainment and enjoyment
